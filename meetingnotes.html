Playoff Scenarios

Division A & B:

  • Wild card Sunday: 6th vs 7th seed, winner plays 5th seed (single elimination)
  • Following Sunday: winner vs 4th seed (single elimination)
  • Then standard playoff scenario
  • Winter league: everyone makes playoffs to keep motivated

Division C & D:

  • Everyone plays each other once, then split
  • Top 5 go to Division C, bottom 6 go to Division D
  • Each division plays another round if season allows
  • Playoffs start after split if season is lagging

Byes

  • Most divisions have odd number of teams, so byes already scheduled
  • Teams allowed one "emergency" bye per season (not enough players)
  • Additional emergency byes require paying both team fees or forfeit
  • No byes in playoffs (only true emergencies considered)
  • In 2 of 3 playoff games: emergency bye gives loss, must be true emergency

Father's Day

  • Teams can opt out of playing on Father's Day
  • Does not count against team byes

Rules Clarifications

Hit-by-Pitch:

  • Umpire's discretion if batter could have avoided being hit
  • Speed of pitch doesn't matter if batter in batter's box
  • Players cannot argue with umpire (same as strike calls)

Double Play Obstruction:

  • Players must slide or run to right field to clear double play
  • Cannot wait until last minute to move
  • Umpires will call automatic double play if obstruction appears close

Dead Ball:

  • Anything in front of plate = dead ball
  • Anything crossing plate = live ball
  • Umpire's call (angle may vary)

Umpires:

  • Some umpires work other leagues; rules will be reviewed with new umpires

Schedules

  • Give advance notice for byes; hard to reschedule after posting
  • Schedule sent out showing teams, byes, and opponents (not time or park)
