About LAMSL

The Latino Men's Softball League (LAMSL) is dedicated to promoting softball among Latino communities in Southern California. Founded in 2005 by Arturo Padilla, Sr., the league provides a fun and competitive environment for players of all skill levels.

Mission

To foster camaraderie, sportsmanship, and community through the game of softball.

History

LAMSL started with a few teams and has grown to include multiple divisions and tournaments throughout the year.
